- These are the most important rules: 
  1. Do not break sentences into two
  2. Do not join independent clauses by a comma
  3. Place a comma before *and*, *but* introducing an independent clause. 
  4.  Enclose parenthetic expressions between commans
  5.  Make the paragraph the unit of composition, one paragraph to each topic
  6.  Omit needless words, sentence should contain not useless words, and paragraph should contain no useless sentences.
